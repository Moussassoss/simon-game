How to Play Simon

1

Watch the sequence of colors that light up

2

Remember the sequence

3

Repeat the sequence by clicking the colors in the same order

4

Each successful round adds one more color to the sequence

5

Make a mistake, and the game is over!

Tips

  • Pay attention to both the colors and sounds
  • Take your time - there's no rush between moves
  • Try to create patterns in your mind to remember longer sequences
